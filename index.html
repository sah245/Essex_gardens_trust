Welcome to Essex Gardens Trust

Preserving and promoting the historic gardens of Essex.

What We Do

We share our love of gardens and landscapes, their design, making, history and the stories that surround them; and we foster that love in others by organising events for the interest and enjoyment of our members and the wider public.

We protect Essex's precious green spaces by identifying and recording their details, making that information available, and influencing future green spaces by providing informed comment on development plans.

We welcome new members to join like-minded people at events and social gatherings. Members receive a twice-yearly Journal and a monthly Newsletter with news and upcoming events.
