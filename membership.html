    Join us

    Become a member of the Essex Gardens Trust and help us preserve and promote the historic gardens of Essex.

    Student Membership

    £25 / year

    Benefits Include:
    • Free or discounted entry to selected gardens
    • Access to our digital newsletter with gardening tips and conservation updates
    • Invitations to student-only events
    • 10% discount on workshops and events
    • Access to online talks & webinars
    • Certificate of support (Great for CVs)
    Join Us

    Individual Membership

    £45 / year

    Benefits Include:
    • Free entry to all partner gardens
    • Seasonal printed + digital newsletter
    • Priority booking for workshops & events
    • 10% discount on classes (e.g. planting, conservation skills)
    • Members-only garden tours and guided walks
    • Access to exclusive gardening resources & planting guides
    • Discounts in partner garden shops/cafés
    • Invitations to special events (e.g. plant fairs, open days)
    Join Us

    Joint Membership

    £60 / year

    Benefits Include:
    • Free entry to all partner gardens for two named individuals
    • Seasonal printed + digital newsletter for both members
    • Priority booking for workshops & events for both members
    • 15% discount on classes (e.g. planting, conservation skills) for both members
    • Members-only garden tours and guided walks for both members
    • Access to exclusive gardening resources & planting guides for both members
    • Discounts in partner garden shops/cafés for both members
    • Invitations to special events (e.g. plant fairs, open days) for both members
